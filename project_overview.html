How It Works

System workflow and data flow

1

Citizen Registration & Login

Citizens register with their details and login to access the complaint submission system.

2

Submit Complaint

Citizens submit grievances with title, description, and category selection.

3

AI Analysis & Routing

System analyzes complaint using AI and routes to the appropriate MP constituency.

4

MP Response

MPs view complaints, analyze with AI, and provide detailed responses.

5

Citizen Notification & Tracking

Citizens receive notifications and can track complaint status in real-time.

6

Admin Monitoring

Admins monitor entire system, manage users, and generate analytics reports.
